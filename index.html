Ar Rahmaan

The Merciful

The One who continually showers all of creation with blessings and prosperity without any disparity. The One who is most kind, loving and merciful.

Mentioned in Quran: 57 times.

+

Ar Raheem

Bestower of Mercy

The Owner of Mercy that encompasses His creations. Ar Rahmaan points to the vastness of Allah's Mercy and Ar Raheem points out to the effect of this Mercy on His creations.

Mentioned in Quran: 114 times.

+

Al Malik

The Eternal Lord

The One who possesses the attribute of Mulk (kingdom), dominion, power, and authority. The One who possesses the disposal of all affairs. The One who has all ruling power over all beings, and is ruled by none.

Mentioned in Quran:
59:23, 20:114, 23:116

+

Al Quddus

The Most Holy

The One who is pure, spotless, without blemish or fault. The One who is far from, and untouched by, worldly imperfections or faults. The One who is beyond all human understanding of purity, perfection and holiness.

Mentioned in Quran: 59:23, 62:1

+

As Salaam

The Source of Peace

The One who is the source of all peace, wholeness and safety. The One who is perfect, whole, prosperous and content. The One who has rendered all of creation to be perfect, whole, prosperous and content.

Mentioned in Quran: 59:23

+

Al Mu'min

Al Mughni

The Bestower of Wealth

The One who gives wealth abundantly. The One who provides all that is needed. The One whose wealth fulfills all needs. The One who enriches all of creation. The One who creates all appearances of independence or self-sufficiency.

Mentioned in Quran: 9:28

+

Al Mani'

The Preventer, The Protector

The One who averts harm, physical or spiritual. The One who guards from harmful situations. The One who prevents undesirable actions. The One who stops one thing from harming another.

+

Ad Dhaar

The Corrector, The Distressor

The One who creates adversity or distress in order to discourage or correct wrongful behavior. The One whose wisdom may use situations that have an outward appearance of being harmful. The One whose wisdom may choose to use forceful corrections.

Mentioned in Quran: 6:17

+

An Nafi'

The Benefiter, The Propitious

The One who helps and who grants all advantages. The One who creates all that produces benefit and usefulness. The One who grants all favorable circumstances. The One who confers all benefits.

Mentioned in Quran: 30:37

+

An Nur

The Light, The Illuminator

The One who is the divine light of all the worlds. The One whose light who illuminates the heart. The One who is the light of wisdom and guidance. The One whose light clarifies, manifests and reveals.

Mentioned in Quran: 24:35

+

Al Haadi

The Guide of the Right Path

The One who continually shows the right way. The One who kindly guides aright. The One who sends prophets and messengers to guide mankind. The One who guides hearts to a knowledge of the Divine essence.

Mentioned in Quran: 22:54

+

Al Badee'

The Wonderful Originator

The marvelous One who originates, commences, invents and creates all that exists, without any model or material. The One who creates in wonderful, awesome, amazingly original ways that have no precedent whatsoever.

Mentioned in Quran: 2:117, 6:101

+

Al Baaqi

The Everlasting, The Eternal

The One who has always existed and who will never cease to be. The One whose existence has neither beginning nor end. The One whose existence is beyond the realm of time.

Mentioned in Quran: 55:27

+

Al Waarith

The Inheritor, The Supreme Heir

The One who remains after all of creation has perished. The One to whom all returns. The One who has everlasting ownership of all that has ever been and that will ever be. The One to whom all possessions return when the possessor is gone.

Mentioned in Quran: 15:23, 57:10

+

Ar Rasheed

The Unerring Director

The One who unerringly decrees, appoints or ordains the right way. The One who is the supreme director to the right path and right belief. The One who perfectly and righteously directs all matters toward their proper conclusion. The One who needs no aid to direct all affairs rightly.

+

As Saboor

The Most Patient

The One who is most patient, steadfast, and enduring. The One who is not moved by haste to carry out any action before it's proper time. The One who patiently endures and does everything in its proper time and proper manner, no matter how long that may take.

Mentioned in Quran:
2:153, 3:200, 103:3
