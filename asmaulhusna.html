Asma Ul Husna

99 Beautiful Names of God

Ar Rahmaan
The Merciful

  • The One who continually showers all of creation with blessings and prosperity without any disparity. The One who is most kind, loving and merciful.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 57 times.

Ar Raheem
Bestower of Mercy

  • The Owner of Mercy that encompasses His creations. Ar Rahmaan points to the vastness of Allah's Mercy and Ar Raheem points out to the effect of this Mercy on His creations.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 114 times.

Al Malik
The Eternal Lord

  • The One who possesses the attribute of Mulk (kingdom), dominion, power, and authority. The One who possesses the disposal of all affairs. The One who has all ruling power over all beings, and is ruled by none.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23, 20:114, 23:116

Al Quddus
The Most Holy

  • The One who is pure, spotless, without blemish or fault. The One who is far from, and untouched by, worldly imperfections or faults. The One who is beyond all human understanding of purity, perfection and holiness.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23, 62:1

As Salaam
The Source of Peace

  • The One who is the source of all peace, wholeness and safety. The One who is perfect, whole, prosperous and content. The One who has rendered all of creation to be perfect, whole, prosperous and content.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23

Al Mu'min
The Source of Faith

  • The One who faithfully bestows the gifts of peace, safety and security. The One who grants freedom from fear. The One who illuminates the heart with faith. The One who is most trustworthy.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23

Al Muhaymin
The Protector, The Guardian

  • The One who ensures well-being. The One who extends wings of Love to cover and protect creation. The One who is ever watchful. The One who protects and guards.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23

Al 'Azeez
The Mighty, The Eminent

  • The One who is the most Powerful and most Cherished. The victorious One whose strength, glory and power are overwhelming and cannot be overcome or resisted. The One who is incomparable and unparalleled.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 3:6, 4:158, 9:40, 48:7, 59:23

Al Jabbar
The Restorer, The Irresistible

  • The One who repairs, reforms and completes. The One who irresistibly compels things to be set aright. The One who compels each and every thing according to divine will, yet is never compelled.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23

Al Mutakabbir
The Supremely Great

  • The One who is supremely great. The One who is greater than all of creation. The One who manifests greatness in all things and in all ways. The One who has rights, privileges and attributes which others have not.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:23

Al Khaaliq
The Creator, Absolute Author

  • The One who determines and creates according to the proper measure and proportion of each thing. The One who plans and determines how, when and where to create. The One who creates something from nothing.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 6:102, 13:16, 36:81, 39:62, 40:62, 59:24

Al Baari'
The Evolver, The Maker from Nothing

  • The One who creates form out of nothing. The One who creates with no model or similarity. The One who sends new forms into existence. The One who evolves and re-creates that which exists, both physically and spiritually.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:24

Al Musawwir
The Fashioner, Bestower of Forms

  • The One who is the fashioner and former of each and every thing in all of creation. The One who has given everything in creation a special inclination or desire. The One who has given everything a special form and a special manner whereby it is distinguished.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 59:24

Al Ghaffar
The All-Forgiving

  • The One who accepts repentance and veils or forgives our faults and sins, time and time again. The One who sets us free from the guilt and shame of our own sins and faults, such that we may discover inner harmony and peace.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 20:82, 38:66, 39:5, 40:42, 71:10

Al Qahhar
The Conqueror, The Prevailer

  • The One who prevails over all of creation. The One who overcomes all obstacles. The One who is victorious over any opposition. The One whose will is irresistible. The One who is master of all. The One to whom all submit.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 12:39, 13:16, 14:48, 38:65, 39:4, 40:16

Al Wahhaab
The Giver of Gifts, Absolute Bestower

  • The One who continually bestows gifts, favors and blessings upon all of creation. The One who is the most generous and liberal giver. The One who gives freely and endlessly, without expectation of any return.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 38:9, 38:35

Ar Razzaaq
The Provider, Bestower of Sustenance

  • The One who creates all means of nourishment and subsistence. The One who is the giver of all things beneficial, both physical and spiritual. The One who provides everything that is needed.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 51:58

Al Fattaah
The Opener, The Revealer

  • The One who opens what is closed. The One who is the judge of what shall be opened. The One by whose guidance that which was closed is opened and the unclear is made clear. The One who lifts veils and who opens the heart.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 34:26

Al 'Aleem
The All-Knowing

  • The One who comprehends everything. The One who knows with certainty. The One whose knowledge of past, present and future is deeply rooted and complete in all respects. The One who is aware of the complete details of all matters. The Omniscient One.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:158, 3:92, 4:35, 24:41, 33:40

Al Qaabid
The Restrainer, The Constrictor

  • The One whose wisdom causes withholding, either physically or spiritually. The One whose wisdom decides when to withhold something, or make something scarce. The One whose wisdom may elect to withhold joy and expansion of the heart.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:245

Al Baasit
The Expander, The Releaser

  • The One who makes ample and plentiful all that is needed. The One who expands and amplifies all abundance. The One who makes the way wide and open. The One whose glory and abundance fill and expand the heart.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:245

Al Khaafidh
The Humbler, The Lowerer

  • The One who chooses the ones to be humbled, softened, made gentle. The One who decides which ones to weaken or diminish. The One who humbles the proud, haughty or insolent, to awaken them from their sleep.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 56:3

Ar Raafi'
The Uplifter, The Exalter

  • The One whose wisdom chooses the ones to be uplifted. The One who uplifts and elevates mankind above petty desires and selfishness. The One who makes it possible to rise above the differences and distinctions that divide mankind.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 58:11, 6:83, 56:3

Al Mu'izz
The Honorer, The Glorifier

  • The One who gives invincible strength and honor. The One who grants honor and power. The One who strengthens and glorifies. The One who makes it possible for someone or something to be respected, cherished and mighty.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 3:26

Al Muzil
The Disgracer, The Humiliator

  • The One whose wisdom creates situations that are low and despicable. The One who creates the appearance of dishonor or degradation. The One whose wisdom produces circumstances of humiliation and disgrace.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 3:26

As Samee'
The All-Hearing, The Ever-Listening

  • The One whose hearing and attention comprehends everything. The One who pays attention to every supplication and invocation. The One who listens to every voice. The One who hears and accepts every word, thought and secret.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:127, 2:256, 8:17, 49:1

Al Baseer
The All-Seeing, The All-Perceiving

  • The One whose insight sees all things clearly, both the apparent and the hidden. The One who sees and understands all that has been, and all that will be. The One who has insight into all things. The One who perceives every detail.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 4:58, 17:1, 42:11, 42:27

Al Hakam
The Judge, The Giver of Justice

  • The One who is the only true judge. The One who always delivers justice in every situation. The One whose judgments and decrees are never over-turned or thwarted. The One who makes the final decision of the nature of all matters. The One who arbitrates all disputes.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 22:69

Al 'Adl
The Just, The Equitable

  • The One who rectifies and sets matters straight in a fair, impartial and equitable manner. The One who always acts justly. The One who delivers absolute justice. The One whose wisdom of justice is based upon complete knowledge of past, present and future.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 6:115

Al Lateef

  • The One who is most subtle and gracious. The One whose nature is gentle, affectionate, courteous and refined. The One whose actions are so fine and subtle that they may be imperceptible, beyond our comprehension.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 22:63, 31:16, 33:34

Al Khabeer
The Knower of Inner Truth

  • The One who knows the internal qualities and meanings of all things. The One who has perfect knowledge and understanding of the true reality of all things. The One who knows and understands the meaning of even the most hidden secrets.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 6:18, 17:30, 49:13, 59:18

Al Haleem
The Most Kind and Gentle

  • The One who is kind, gracious and serene in all situations. The One who is calm and deliberate, never hasty, even with the rebellious and wrongdoers. The One who gives us the opportunity and situations to learn to be kind, gracious and patient.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:235, 17:44, 22:59, 35:41

Al 'Atheem
The Most Grand, The Greatest

  • The One who is greatest, mightiest, grandest and above all. The One who is of the greatest importance. The One who is magnificent, vast and most revered. The One who is of perfect and absolute greatness.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:255, 42:4, 56:96

Al Ghafoor
The Forgiving, The Pardoner

  • The One who completely forgives our sins and faults. The One who accepts repentance and pardons sins and faults. The One who veils or forgives our faults and sins such that they are not seen by anyone else, not even the angels.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:173, 8:69, 16:110, 41:32

Ash Shakoor
The Most Appreciative

  • The One who is most thankful for righteousness and bestows great rewards for good deeds. The One who gratefully gives large rewards, even for a small amount of good work. The One who acknowledges and bountifully rewards all acts of goodness, thankfulness and praise.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 35:30, 35:34, 42:23, 64:17

Al 'Alee
The Exalted, The Most High

  • The One Who is above and exceeds all others. The Most High, above whom there is nothing higher. The One whose rank and station are the Most High. The One who is above and surpasses all that has ever been, all that there now is, and all that shall ever be.

    42:51, 34:23

Al Hafeedh

Al Muqeet

The Fullfiller of Prayers

Al Waasi'
The All-Embracing

  • The One whose expansive essence holds and embraces all of creation. The One who is ample-giving, all-embracing and all-pervading. The One whose power, knowledge, mercy and generosity are boundless.

Al Hakeem

As Saboor
The Most Patient

  • The One who is most patient, steadfast, and enduring. The One who is not moved by haste to carry out any action before it's proper time. The One who patiently endures and does everything in its proper time and proper manner, no matter how long that may take.

  • Mentioned in Quran: 2:153, 3:200, 103:3
